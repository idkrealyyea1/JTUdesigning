How We Create

From Sketch to Story

Sketch & Design

Every rug begins as a hand-drawn concept inspired by landscapes and nature.

Choose Yarn

100% New Zealand wool, hand-selected for texture, sheen and color richness.

Color Selection

Colors are mixed and matched to achieve the exact landscape palette envisioned.

Tufting

Each loop and cut pile is placed by hand using a tufting gun, row by row.

Carving

The pile is sculpted to create depth, dimension and the layered landscape effect.

Cleaning

A thorough wash to bring out the wool's natural softness and luster.

Finishing

Fringe is trimmed, edges are bound, and the piece is inspected for perfection.

Delivery

Carefully rolled, wrapped in linen, and delivered to your door — or ready to hang.

Our Story

Roots Woven in Every Thread

Every rug we create is a handmade original. No machines, no shortcuts — just skilled hands, premium wool, and a deep respect for the craft of tufting.

We draw our designs from the earth itself: the layered strata of desert rock, the flowing contours of mountain valleys, the quiet palette of a dawn sky.

Every thread has meaning. Every color is chosen with intention. Every piece is unique — a small landscape you can walk on, hang, or live with.
